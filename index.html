Our Services
Structured Advisory Across
Every Tax Dimension

Whether you need routine compliance or complex restructuring, Capitalstance delivers with precision and strategic depth.

01

Tax Compliance

Full management of VAT returns, PAYE deductions, Corporate Income Tax, and all KRA filing obligations. We ensure timely submissions, accurate calculations, and penalty-free compliance for your business across all tax heads.

02

Tax Planning & Structuring

Strategic tax planning for directors, investors, and growing businesses. We identify legal avenues to minimize tax exposure through entity structuring, transaction design, and incentive optimization — all within Kenya Revenue Authority guidelines.

03

SME Advisory

Practical, results-oriented advisory for small and medium enterprises. From financial structuring and governance setup to growth planning, we provide the advisory infrastructure that SMEs need but rarely access.

04

KRA Support & Dispute Handling

Expert handling of KRA audits, tax assessments, objections, and tribunal matters. We represent your interests professionally, negotiate on your behalf, and ensure fair resolution through proper legal and administrative channels.

05

Payroll & Statutory Filing

Complete payroll outsourcing including PAYE computation, NHIF and NSSF deductions, payslip generation, and monthly remittances. Fully compliant with the Employment Act and current statutory rates.

06

Company Setup Advisory

End-to-end guidance for business registration, optimal entity structuring (LLC, partnership, branch), PIN/VAT registration, and building your compliance framework from day one. Start right, grow right.
